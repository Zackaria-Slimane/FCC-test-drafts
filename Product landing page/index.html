           TWO VINEYARDS ... ONE PASSION

Winemaker at Château de la FONT DU LOUP in CHÂTEAU NEUF DU PAPE since 1977 and keeping the pioneering spirit of my ancestors living in Algeria since 1830, I created a new vineyard in Essaouira in 1994 in Morocco in the ESSAOUIRA region until 'then virgin of any vines. It will take 4 long years to clear, stone, dig, the wells and gradually plant the vineyard, build the cellar, its buildings to achieve the property. Backing onto the desert and benefiting from the trade winds, the DOMAINE DU VAL D’ARGAN is the first and only Moroccan vineyard exclusively dedicated to grape varieties from the Rhône valley. All the diversity, the richness of the Rhone vineyards can be found today magnificently represented in southern Morocco, a new land of great wines.
